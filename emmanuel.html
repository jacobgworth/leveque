Meet Emmanuel and family

Emmanuel is a farmer who dreams of having a house one day.

He, his wife Enia, and their two children Emmanuela (10) and Eniel (8) have lived in the tent since the earthquake, when their house was destroyed. That means over 5 years of living in conditions unsuitable for anyone - especially young kids.

Now, they face heat inside the tent and as a result, their family is frequently sick.

For Emmanuel and Enia, a new home is a safe, hope-filled, and healthy environment to raise their children.  

With a new home, “I will feel so comfortable”. He expresses that he will be incredibly grateful to all those involved in changing the life trajectory for his family.
